Bios

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is the lead singer and guitarist of the band. He started playing music when he was 12 years old and formed his first band in high school. He is influenced by bands like Nirvana, Radiohead, and Daft Punk. He writes most of the lyrics for the band and likes to experiment with different sounds and effects.

Larry Cement

Larry Cement is the bassist and backing vocalist of the band. He met Harvey at a local music festival and joined the band in 2020. He has a background in classical music and plays the cello and piano as well. He helps with the arrangements and production.

Tommy Cement

Tommy Cement is the drummer and electronic wizard of the band. He is Larry's younger brother and has been playing drums since he was 8 years old. He is a fan of genres like techno and adds a lot of energy and groove to the band's live shows.
